Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

MasseurMatch ("we", "us") is an online directory platform that helps users connect with LGBTQ+ affirming massage therapists. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information. MasseurMatch is owned by X RankFlow Media Group LLC.

Important: MasseurMatch is a directory only. We do not provide massage services, bookings or payment processing. We simply list therapists and allow users to discover them.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

We collect the following information you give us directly:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile information
  • Images uploaded to your profile
  • Communication preferences
  • Messages sent to support

2.2 Automatically collected information

We collect certain information automatically when you use our site:

  • IP address
  • Device information and browser type
  • Usage statistics
  • Cookies and tracking data
  • Referral data

2.3 Sensitive information

MasseurMatch does not collect sensitive information such as sexual orientation, medical information or massage preferences, unless you voluntarily provide it for your profile. If you choose to disclose any sensitive information, you do so at your own discretion.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Send verification codes
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the platform
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Maintain security and moderation
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. SMS/Messaging use

When you provide your phone number, you consent to receive transactional messages such as account verification codes, profile updates, security alerts and customer support replies. We do not send marketing messages without explicit opt‑in. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance reply HELP. Message and data rates may apply.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for authentication, session management, security, analytics and user customization. You can control cookies through browser settings, but disabling cookies may impact your experience.

6. Legal basis (GDPR)

For users in the EU/UK, our lawful bases for processing your data are one or more of the following: performance of a contract, legitimate interest, consent and compliance with legal obligations.

7. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Hosting providers
  • Analytics platforms
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment or billing tools (if added in future)
  • Legal authorities when required

All vendors are contractually required to protect your data and comply with applicable regulations.

8. Security

We employ industry‑standard security measures including encryption, access controls, secure data storage, regular audits and moderation.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export your data, opt out of SMS or limit processing. To exercise these rights contact us at support@masseurmatch.com.

10. Data retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as required by law.

11. Children’s privacy

MasseurMatch is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through the website. Your continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any updates.
